State expansion model

A weighted scoring framework evaluating 50 states across 5 factors to identify priority markets for Virtual Elderly Care Platform's next expansion phase.

Scoring method — Max 100: Each component score is calculated by taking the state with the highest observed value as 100. All other states are scored proportionally against that maximum. The weighted composite is the sum of each component score multiplied by its weight.

Key assumptions & scope

Service scope

Virtual Elderly Care Platform is modeled as a digital care coordination and resource navigation service for elderly individuals living alone who lack a reliable informal support network.

Greenfield analysis

Treats Virtual Elderly Care Platform as a net-new startup — no carry-over from existing the platform business, brand, or infrastructure.

Traditional Medicare only

Scoped exclusively to Original Medicare (Part B). Model should be refined if expanding to Medicare Advantage or commercial plans.
